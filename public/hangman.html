HANGMAN

HOW TO PLAY: The blank spaces below represent a word. A rough translation of that word is provided for you. In order to play, press a letter on your keyboard. If the guessed letter appears in the word, it will display in place of the appropriate blank. If that letter does not appear in the word, your guesses remaining will decrement by one. If you guess all the missing letters correctly, your wins increase by one. If you run out of remaining guesses, your losses increase by one. Once you have reached 10 wins, you have completed the level!
