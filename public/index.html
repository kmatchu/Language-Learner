A million ways to say 'Hello.'

We may live far apart, but our words connect us. As the world connects more and more, never miss a chance for an adventure or a new friend. Grab a hold of life, take the dive, and try out a new language. The world will open to you - as long as you know how to say 'Hello!'

Learn quick - while having fun!

The game rules are easy - but don't underestimate tackling a new language. You can adjust the difficulty, and check out new words each time you play a game. Children, students and adults all can enjoy games of jumble, wordsearch and hangman, with more games in future updates.
